of the magnetic moment and the concept of the loss cone.

180 3.0 0.0 dialog
The electron bounces back and forth between the two
mirror points. As it moves into stronger field, its
perpendicular velocity increases (to conserve the
magnetic moment mu = mv_perp^2 / 2B), and eventually
all the kinetic energy is in the perpendicular direction.
At that point, the parallel velocity reaches zero and
the particle reflects.
end_dialog
